Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Fettuccine Noodles
  • Alfredo Sauce
  • Salt/Pepper
  • Cheese
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Boil water.
  2. Cook chicken and and garlic.
  3. Place Noodles into water and follow instructions on package.
  4. Put sauce into the same pan as chicken and heat.
  5. Drain noodles.
  6. Add chicken and sauce to noodles. Top with cheese.
  7. Enjoy!
