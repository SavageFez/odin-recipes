Smoked Ribs

Smoked ribs are great for Saturday and Sunday footballs games! Follow this easy recipe to make the juciest ribs!

Ingredients

  • 1 Full Rack of Ribs
  • Mustard
  • Seasoning Rub
  • BBQ Sauce
  • Apple Vinegar
  • Water

Steps

  1. Coat the rack of ribs throughly with your choice of mustard.
  2. Spread your choice of rub ontop of the mustard.
  3. Let the meat sit while turning on the smoker and setting it to a low temperature.
  4. Once the smoker is to the right temperature, place the ribs inside.
  5. Check back occasionally and spray a mix of apple vinegar and water on the ribs to keep them moist.
  6. After about two hours, take the ribs about and cover them in your choice of BBQ.
  7. Wrap the ribs inside foil and place them back into the smoker.
  8. Once the ribs are around 190 degrees, take them out and let them cool for at least 30 minutes.
  9. Enjoy!
